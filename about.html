Dr. Mustapha Bukar Gana

Chief Lecturer at Umar Ibn Ibrahim El-Kanemi College of Education Science & Technology, Bama, Borno State, Nigeria

Masters in Guidance and Counselling, Lecturer of Guidance and Counselling Unit, Psychology Department

Biography

Dr. Mustapha Bukar Gana was once a Head of Department Primary Education Studies (1992 to 1995), Coordinator Pre-NCE Programme (1995 to 1999), Deputy Provost (1995 to 1999), and Provost of the College (1999 to 2003). His current field of specialization is PhD in Distance Education and e-Learning. He is a trained online course creation, content creation for online courses, instruction designer for online learning, e-Learning technologist and LMS/VLE expertise. He currently uses Google Classroom VLE to deliver his PGDE Guidance and Counselling course to his PGDE students.

He has authored many peer-reviewed articles in local, national, and international journals and 3 eBooks and 1 edited eBook on Amazon UK, and has 2 manuscripts awaiting publication with Europe Books and OPENTECH.
